Cure Plus Hospital, Bishnupur is a healthcare hospital located in Bishnupur, Manipur. The hospital provides medical services including general medicine, surgery, pediatrics, gynecology, orthopedics, ENT, ophthalmology, psychiatry, and emergency care.
