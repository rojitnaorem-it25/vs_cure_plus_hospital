About Cure Plus Hospital :

History of Cure Plus Hospital :

Founded in 1953 by Konjengbam Thoiba Meitei, Cure Plus Hospital was established with the vision of providing quality and affordable healthcare services to the community. What began as a small healthcare center gradually grew into a trusted hospital known for compassionate patient care, modern medical facilities, and experienced doctors. Over the years, Cure Plus Hospital has continued to expand its medical services, advanced treatments, and healthcare technology while maintaining its commitment to patient safety and excellence. Today, the hospital serves patients with dedicated departments, skilled specialists, emergency care, and personalized treatment solutions, carrying forward the founder’s mission of improving healthcare for everyone.

Dr.Konjengbam Thoiba Meitei

( Founder & Head of Neurology Department – Cure Plus Hospital )

Dr. Konjengbam Thoiba Meitei is a dedicated healthcare professional and the visionary founder of Cure Plus Hospital. With a strong commitment to patient care and medical excellence, he has played an important role in developing quality healthcare services since 1953. As the Head of the Neurology Department, he specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders related to the brain, nerves, and nervous system.
